Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 250g lasagne sheet
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 250ml beef stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250g ricotta
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Beef Sauce:
    • In a large skillet or pan, heat a drizzle of oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saute until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
    • Add the minced beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Tomato Base:
    • Stir in the canned chopped tomatoes and tomato paste.
    • Add the dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, and sugar. Mix well.
    • Pour in the beef stock. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Simmer the sauce for about 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavours to develop and the sauce to thicken.
  3. Assemble the Lasagne:
    • Preheat your oven to 180 degrees.
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the beef sauce at the bottom.
    • Place a layer of lasagne sheets over the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta over the sheets.
    • Sprinkle a portion of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the ricotta.
  4. Repeat Layers:
    • Continue layering with more beef sauce, sheets, ricotta and grated cheeses.
    • Repeat the layering process until you've used up all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  5. Bake the Lasagne:
    • Cover the baking dish with tin foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Serve:
    • Once the lasagne is cooked and the cheese is melted, remove it from the oven.
    • Allow the lasagne to cool slightly before slicing.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Enjoy:
    • Serve the delicious Lasagne with a side salad and crusty bread for a hearty and satisfying meal.
