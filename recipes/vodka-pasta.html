Vodka Pasta Recipe

Description

Indulge in the delightful combination of flavors with this creamy vodka pasta recipe. Al dente penne pasta is paired with a rich and velvety tomato cream sauce infused with a hint of vodka. The sauce is prepared by saut√©ing garlic and shallots, then simmering tomatoes, heavy cream, and vodka to create a luscious base. A sprinkle of red pepper flakes adds a gentle kick of heat, while fresh basil leaves lend a fragrant herbaceousness.

Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, allowing it to soak up the delicious flavors. Serve the creamy vodka pasta with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an irresistible, elegant, and comforting dish that's perfect for special occasions or a cozy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces (340g) penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Shallot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 Vodka
  • 1 Cup Double Cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/4 Fresh Basil
  • Grated Parmesan for serving
