Vodka Pasta Recipe

Indulge in the delightful combination of flavors with this creamy vodka pasta recipe. Al dente penne pasta is paired with a rich and velvety tomato cream sauce infused with a hint of vodka. The sauce is prepared by sautéing garlic and shallots, then simmering tomatoes, heavy cream, and vodka to create a luscious base. A sprinkle of red pepper flakes adds a gentle kick of heat, while fresh basil leaves lend a fragrant herbaceousness.

Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, allowing it to soak up the delicious flavors. Serve the creamy vodka pasta with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an irresistible, elegant, and comforting dish that's perfect for special occasions or a cozy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces (340g) penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Shallot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 Vodka
  • 1 Cup Double Cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/4 Fresh Basil
  • Grated Parmesan for serving

Recipe

  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped shallot. Sauté for about 2 minutes, until fragrant and slightly softened.
  3. Add Flavours: Sprinkle in the red pepper flakes and stir to combine with the garlic and shallot.
  4. Tomato Base: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir well. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavours to get to know eachother and for the sauce to slightly reduce.
  5. Vodka Addition: Carefully add the vodka to the sauce. Allow it to simmer for another 5 minutes, giving time for the alcohol to cook off and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  6. Creamy Goodness: Lower the heat and pour in the heavy cream. Stir gently to combine and let the sauce simmer for an additional 5 minutes.Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Incorporate Basil: Stir in the chopped basil leaves, reserving some for the garnish. The fresh basil will add a burst of flavour and aroma to the sauce.
  8. Combine with Pasta: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing gently to coat the pasta evenly with the cream tomato vodka sauce. Allow the pasta to heat through for a few minutes.
  9. Serve: Dish out the creamy vodka pasta onto serving plates. Garnish with additional chopped basil and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
  10. Enjoy: Serve the Vodka Pasta while it's still warm and comforting. Pair it with a side saland and some crusty bread for a complete and satisfying meal.
